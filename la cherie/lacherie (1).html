5-Day Sample Itinerary

DAY 1: Arrival & Promenade

Hotel check-in, followed by an evening stroll along the beautiful Promenade Walk in White Town.

DAY 2: French Quarter Heritage

Explore the architectural elegance of the French Quarter, Notre Dame Cathedral, and Our Lady of Angels Church.

DAY 3: Spiritual & Market Visit

Visit ancient Temples, the serene Matrimandir (Auroville), and local markets for cultural insights.

DAY 4: Auroville & Beach Cruising

Full day in Auroville, visit Serenity Beach, and enjoy a relaxing coastal cruise.

DAY 5: Beach Fun & Departure

Morning at the beach with optional water sports, a farewell sunset party, and final check-out.

Quick Tips for Travelers

  • Location is Key: Choose your hotel near the French Quarter for best access to sites.
  • Rent a Bike: White Town is congested. We recommend walking or hiring a bike for easy movement.
  • Stay Hydrated: The coastal weather requires you to keep yourself fully hydrated at all times.
  • Matrimandir Slot: Book your meditation slot at the Matrimandir 2-3 days in advance.
  • Try Surfing: Take advantage of the fantastic local beaches and try a surfing session.
  • Dessert Hunt: Try the incredible desserts at every cafe/restaurant you visit!
